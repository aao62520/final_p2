Lifting

Lifting can involve a variety of equipment, including dumbbells, barbells, resistance bands, weight machines, and bodyweight exercises. The primary goal of lifting is to challenge your muscles to the point of fatigue, which stimulates the growth and repair of muscle fibers, leading to increased strength, tone, and overall fitness. Lifting can be done by people of all ages and fitness levels, and can provide a range of health benefits when done properly and consistently.

Food

You are what you eat. Therefore nutrition is very important during your lifting journey. You should typically aim to eat your protein in bodyweight, and cut out things such as processed sugar and sweetened beverages.
