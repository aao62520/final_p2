About Me


My name is Ahmad Odeh, president of Odeh Nutrition. I'm currently a freshman at NJIT majoring in Human Computer Interaction. During my freetime, I enjoy working out, playing video games, driving, and watching football. While working out started as a chore for me, it ended up growing into one of my main passion, and something I look forward to on a 6 day basis. I started 10% nutrition to help out people who are just starting to get into fitness. I chose the name in regard of the 10% of the population who are willing to do whatever it takes to reach their goals.
