Splits

Push Pull Legs

Push Pull Legs is a 6 day split that goes in 3 day intervals. Day 1 trains chest, triceps, and shoulders. Day 2 is back and biceps, Day 3 is legs, then repeat until your 1 rest day.

Push Pull legs X Arnold

The Push Pull Legs Arnold Split is a workout routine that's popular among fitness enthusiasts and bodybuilders. It's named after Arnold Schwarzenegger, who used a similar workout regimen during his bodybuilding career. It follows the standard push pull legs routine, but after the 3rd day you train arms, then chest and back, and a regular leg day after.

Full Body split

A 3-day full body split is a workout routine that targets all major muscle groups in three separate workout sessions per week. Each session typically includes exercises that work multiple muscle groups in order to provide an effective full-body workout.
